About the To-Do List App

Home Settings Task Details

Welcome to the To-Do List app! This app is designed to help you stay organized and track your tasks with ease. You can mark tasks as complete, see your progress, and navigate to other useful pages like Settings and Task Details.

With a clean and intuitive design, this app allows you to manage your daily tasks efficiently. Although some features, like adding tasks or deleting them, require JavaScript, the layout and style of the app remain functional and visually appealing.

Stay productive and organized!
